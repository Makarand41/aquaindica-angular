Who We Are

Aquaindica Techno Solutions Private Limited, is formed to provide customized,
and comprehensive solutions by adopting latest technologies in upgradation of pumping systems, irrigation, water & wastewater treatment, water quality management, controlling water loss & non-revenue water, applications of non-chemical methods such as nano bubbles, ultrasound technologies in water/wastewater treatment and Conversion of Intermittent to Continuous equitable Water Supply by direct pumping under Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT 2.0) program.

We offer customised coating solutions for pumps, blowers, fans to retain, regain efficiency and sustainable performance. Specially designed skid-based booster pumping systems for fast-track projects. PPP based contracts for long-term Operation and Maintenance of water supply schemes. We also conduct upskilling programs such as trainings and technical workshop for capacity building for water utilities.
